JUMPING JACKS

Start with your feet together and your arms by your sides,
then jump up with your feet apart and your hands overhead.

Return to the start position then do the next rep. This
exercise provides a full body workout and works all your
large muscles group.
Do it for 20 sec. 2 times

ABDOMINIAL CRUNCHES

Lie on your feet back with your knees bent and your
arms streched forward.

Then lift your upper body off the floor. Hold for a few
seconds and slowly return .
It primarily works the rectus abdominis muscles and
the obliques.
Do 2 sets of 12 times.

RUSSIAN TWIST

Sit on the floor with your knees bent ,feet lifted
a little bit and back tilted backward.

Then hold your hands together and twist from side to side
Do 2 sets of 2 times.

MOUNTAIN CLIMBER

Start in the push-up position Bend your right knee toward your
chest and keep your left leg straight, then quickly switch
from one leg to the other.
This exercise strengthnes multiple muscles groups.
Do 2 set of 16 times.

HEEL TOUCH

Lie on the ground with your legs bent and your arms by your sides.

Slightly lift your upper body off the floor and make your hands
alternately reach your heels.
Do 2 set of 16 times.

LEG RAISE

Lie down on your back, and put your hands beneath your hips for support.

Then lift your leg up until they form a right angle with the floor.

Slowly bring your leg back down and repeat the exercise.
Do 2 set of 16 times.
