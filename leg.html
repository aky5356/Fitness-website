Leg Workout !!Let's Do it

SIDE HOP

Stand on the floor ,put your hand in
front of you and hop from side to side.
Do it for 30sec.







Video for your refernce

SQUATS

Stand with your feet shoulder width apart and your arm stretched forward ,then lower your body until your thighs are parallel with the floor.
Your knees should be extended in the same direction as your toes. Return to the start position and do the next rep.
This works the thighs ,hips buttocks ,quads ,hamstrings and lower body.
Do it 24 times in 2 set.

SIDE LYING LEG LIFT LEFT

Lie down on your side with your head rested on your right arm. Lift your upper leg up and return to the start position.

Make sure your left leg goes stright up and down during the excercise.
It's great excercise for the gluteus.

Do it 12 times in 2 set.

BACKWARD LUNGE

Stand with your feet shoulder width apart and your hand on your hips.

Step a big step backward with your right leg and lower your body until your left thigh is parallel to the floor.Return and repeat with the other side.

Do it 2 sets of 14 times.

DONKEY KICKS LEFT

Start on all four with your knees under your butt and your hand under your shoulders.

Then lift your left leg and squeezze your butt as much as you can go.Go back to the start of position and repeat the exercise. Do it 2 set of 16 times.
