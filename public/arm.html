ARM RAISE

Stand on the floor with your arms extended straight forward at shoulder height.

Raise your arms above your head.Return to the start position and repeat.
Do it for 30sec.

SIDE ARM RAISE

Stand with your feet shoulders width apart.
Raise your arms to the sides at shoulders height,then put them down.
Repeat the exercise. Keep your arms staright during the exercise.
Do it for 30 sec.



Video for your refence

TRICEPS DIPS

For the start position, sit on the chair.Then move your hip off the chair with your hands holding the edge of the chair.
Slowly bend and stretch your arms to make your body go up and down.This is a great exercise for the triceps.
Repeat it for 10 times.

DIAMOND PUSH-UPS

Start in the push-up position.Make a diamond shape with your forefingers and thumbs together under your chest.
Then push your body up and down.Remember to keep your body straight.
repeat it for 6 times atleast.



Video for your refence

CHEST PRESS PULSE

Hold your forearms together at shoulders height and bend your elbows with your hands together to make an L shape.

Then lift your forearms up and down.
Repeat it for 16 sec.

PUNCHES

Stand with one of your leg forward and your knees bent slightly.Bend your elbows and clench your fists in front of your face.
Extend one arm forward with the palm facing the floor. Take the arm back and repeat with the other arm.
Try to do it for atleast 30sec.
